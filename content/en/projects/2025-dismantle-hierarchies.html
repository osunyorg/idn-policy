Governance & Geopolitics Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Collective direct action – challenging corporate and state control 💪🏽

💡 Translation: Disrupt business-as-usual power structures.🚫 Organise general consumption strikes to resist economic coercion.✊ Support worker takeovers of metabolic vehicles (ports, airports, highways, etc.), ensuring key infrastructure serves people, not profits.💥 Power should flow from the bottom up, not be imposed from the top down.
