Policy Objective - Dismantling systemic injustice

Dismantling systemic injusticeJustice systems should repair harm, not perpetuate it. This policy ensures marginalised communities receive redress and power over decisions affecting them.

What changes?
xxx

Inequality Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Legal and policy reforms – dismantling institutional discrimination 📜

💡 Translation: No more racist, sexist, or classist laws keeping people down.⚖ Reform laws to guarantee equal opportunities in education, jobs, and politics.🏛 End legal frameworks that uphold discrimination and exclusion.📢 Ensure fair representation in decision-making bodies.

2 - Community empowerment – shifting power to the people 💪🏿

💡 Translation: Justice means giving people real control over their futures.🏡 Support community-led decision-making.👥 Ensure marginalised groups have direct influence over policies that affect them.🌱 Fund grassroots initiatives that build autonomy and resilience.

3 - Restorative practices – repair, don’t (only) penalise 🛠️

💡 Translation: Justice should heal, not punish.🤝 Use restorative justice to address harms and conflicts.🏚 Invest in trauma-informed support systems instead of criminalisation.🏛 Ensure affected communities shape policies on accountability and healing.💥 Justice means addressing past harms and creating conditions for real equality.
