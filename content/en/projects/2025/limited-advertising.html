Policy Objective 1 - Reducing consumerist influence

Advertising fuels unnecessary consumption and manipulates people into buying things they don’t need. This policy scales back advertising’s reach and influence.

Production & Consumption Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Regulate advertising content 😎

💡 Translation:  Stop promoting wasteful and destructive consumption. 🚫 Ban advertising for harmful industries—fast food, SUVs, fossil fuels, predatory loans. 📉 Restrict advertising that promotes high-consumption lifestyles.

Policy Objective 2 - Ban it
2 - Ban advertising in public spaces 🏖️

💡 Translation:  Let’s reclaim public spaces from corporate propaganda. 🏙 Remove billboards and corporate ads from cityscapes. 🎭 Replace ads with public art, cultural projects, and community messaging."

Policy Objective 3 - Tax it
1 - Tax advertising of unsustainable products 💰

💡 Translation:  Make wasteful and manipulative ads expensive to make. 💰 Tax advertisements for fast food, aviation, automobiles, and fossil fuels. 💥 A just economy doesn’t need to manipulate people into overconsumption.
