Policy Objective - Sufficiency over wastefulness

Energy demand continues to rise despite efficiency gains, largely because growth cancels out savings. This policy promotes a radical shift toward sufficiency—doing more with less, rather than chasing endless growth.

Energy & Environment Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Energy efficiency improvements ⚡

💡 Translation:  Use energy smarter, not just more. 🏠 Better-insulated buildings to reduce heating/cooling needs. 🚆 Efficient transport policies to cut unnecessary energy waste. 🏭 Industrial energy reduction strategies to lower demand.

2 - Promote energy sufficiency 🔋

💡 Translation:  Less overall energy use, not ""green growth." 🌍 Encourage cultural and lifestyle shifts toward lower energy living. 🏡 Policies that support local, small-scale, and low-energy solutions. 📉 Reduce unnecessary consumption in homes, businesses, and industries.
