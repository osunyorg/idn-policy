Policy Objective - Defund war, dismantle corporate influence. No war but the class war

Military expansion and corporate lobbying funnel resources away from public well-being and into systems of domination and control. This policy seeks to redirect power and money away from war and corporate elites.

Governance & Geopolitics Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Regulate lobbying – cut corporate influence in politics 👨‍💼

💡 Translation:  Governments should serve people, not billionaires. 💰 Cap or abolish political donations to eliminate corporate control of politics. 🚪 End the revolving door between politics and business. 📰 Invest in independent local journalism and break up media conglomerates.

2 - Demilitarise security – shift from repression to care 🔐

💡 Translation:  Replace military and police dominance with community-led alternatives. 🛑 Abolish the prison-industrial complex and demilitarise police forces. 🏡 Invest in crisis response teams, community care, and education instead of policing. ⚖️ Decriminalise survival (e.g., homelessness, subsistence work, informal economies).

3 - Reduce military spending – defund war, fund liberation and life 💣

💡 Translation:  Stop using public money for war, ethnic cleansing, genocide, destruction. 💣 Significantly reduce national military budgets. 🔫 Ban or strictly regulate arms trade, especially in conflict zones. 🌱 Redirect military funding toward peacebuilding and environmental protection. 💥 A just society prioritises care, not control and bloodshed.
