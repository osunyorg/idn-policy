Governance & Geopolitics Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Democratise financial institutions 🏦

💡 Translation: The IMF and World Bank should serve people, not creditors.🌍 Reform governance structures so Global South nations have equal decision-making power.💰 End coercive lending policies that enforce austerity and resource extraction.

2 - Abolish veto power in the UN ❎

💡 Translation: No country should have the power to block global action.🚫 End the UN Security Council’s veto system, which allows powerful nations to dominate global decisions.🌍 Ensure international decision-making is democratic and accountable to all.

3 - Increase transparency and accountability 🔦

💡 Translation: No more backroom deals in international governance.🔍 Introduce mechanisms for civil society oversight of global institutions.🏛 Ensure transnational corporations and NGOs are held accountable for their actions.💥 Global governance should be built on justice, not control.
