Policy Objective

Prioritising need-based production over profit-driven wasteInstead of mass-producing useless and harmful products, this policy shifts production toward goods and services that actually improve lives and ecosystems.

What changes?
xxx

Production & Consumption Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Encourage worker-owned production systems 🧑🏿‍🏭

💡 Translation: Factories and businesses should serve people, not bosses or investors.🏭 Support cooperatives and worker-owned businesses that prioritise social needs over profit.🔄 Shift economic incentives away from endless growth and toward community well-being.

2 - Dismantle large corporations 🔨

💡 Translation: No one needs more SUVs, but we do need better public transport.🚗 Scale down or ban harmful industries—SUVs, arms, fast food, fossil fuels, advertising.🔍 Regulate monopolies that exploit workers, extract resources, and concentrate wealth.

3 - Direct activism and sabotage against harmful industries ✊🏽

💡 Translation: When governments won’t act, the people will.✊ Support strategic sabotage against exploitative industries.💻 Promote digital resistance, like anti-capitalist malware programs exposing corporate abuses.💥 Production should be about meeting needs, not creating profit-driven excess.
