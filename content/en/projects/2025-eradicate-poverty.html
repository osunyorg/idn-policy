Policy Objective - Meeting everyone’s fundamental needs

Poverty is not a personal failing—it is the result of policy decisions and choices. This policy guarantees/aims universal access to essential resources, so no one is left behind.

Inequality Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Universal basic services – essentials should not be for profit 🏥

💡 Translation:  No one should go without healthcare, housing, or education. 🏥 Make healthcare, education, housing, nutritious food, public transport, and internet free at the point of use. 🏡 Publicly fund affordable housing, ending homelessness. 🚆 Expand high-quality, free public transport networks.

2 - Basic income schemes – financial security for all 💰

💡 Translation:  Everyone deserves a guaranteed income, not just the wealthy. 💰 Provide universal or unconditional basic income programmes. 📉 Reduce dependency on exploitative low-wage jobs. 🏛 Ensure income security beyond the wage system.

3 - Employment programmes – work that serves society, not just profits 🧑‍🤝‍🧑

💡 Translation:  Create jobs that benefit people and the planet. 🌱 Fund jobs in care work, ecological restoration, and public infrastructure. ⚖ Prioritise employment in sustainable industries over extractive ones. 🚀 Ensure fair wages and dignified working conditions. 💥 Eradicating poverty isn’t about charity—it’s about restructuring economies to serve people, not profits.
