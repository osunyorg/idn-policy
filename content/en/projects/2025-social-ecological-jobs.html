Policy Objective - Shifting employment towards sustainability

A post-growth economy needs jobs that restore ecosystems, strengthen communities, and build resilience. This policy prioritises work that sustains both people and the planet.

Work Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Train workers for the green economy 💚

💡 Translation:  Shift skills and training toward regenerative work. 🌱 Invest in training programs for ecological and low-impact work. 🛠 Support skill-sharing in traditional crafts, repair work, and community farming. 📚 Fund not-for-profit and community education in sustainability.

2 - Promote ecological restoration and community energy jobs 🌼

💡 Translation:  Create jobs that repair, not destroy. 🌍 Fund large-scale ecological restoration projects (reforestation, wetland protection, urban greening). ☀️ Support small-scale, community-owned renewable energy projects. 🏡 Train workers for decentralised, low-impact energy systems. 💥 Work should heal, not exploit—employment must serve both people and the planet.
