Policy Objective

🌍 Farming that regenerates, not depletesIndustrial agriculture maximises short-term yields at the cost of long-term soil health, biodiversity, and sustainability. This policy promotes regenerative and diverse farming methods that enhance ecosystems rather than destroy them.

What changes?
Promote small-scale farming

Food Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Institutionalise artisanal and organic farming 👩‍🌾

💡 Translation: Support farming methods that work with nature, not against it.🌾 Promote organic farming, permaculture, and agroecology.🌍 Encourage farming that builds soil fertility and biodiversity.🚫 Reduce reliance on chemical inputs like pesticides and synthetic fertilisers.

2 - Support for small-scale farmers 🧑‍🌾

💡 Translation: Shift resources from agribusiness to peasant and local farmers.🏡 Provide training, financial aid, and market access for small farmers.🌱 Support urban gardening, local food co-ops, and permaculture streets.🛒 Strengthen community-based food distribution systems.
