Policy Objective 1 - Farming that regenerates, not depletes

Industrial agriculture maximises short-term yields at the cost of long-term soil health, biodiversity, and sustainability. This policy promotes regenerative and diverse farming methods that enhance ecosystems rather than destroy them.
