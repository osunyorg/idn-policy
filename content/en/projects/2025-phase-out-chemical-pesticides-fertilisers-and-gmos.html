Policy Objective
Food Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - Dismantle chemical dependency and corporate control over farming 🚜

💡 Translation: Translation: Farming should work with nature, not against it.🛑 Ban synthetic pesticides and fertilisers that degrade soil, poison water, and harm pollinators.🌾 Establish farmer-led seed banks and strengthen protections for non-GMO, heirloom, and indigenous crop varieties.💰 Redirect subsidies away from chemical-intensive agribusiness toward regenerative, organic, and agroecological practices.🏛 Introduce strict regulations against GMO seed patents, ensuring that farmers retain control over their seeds instead of being forced into corporate dependency.🥦 Expand research and public investment in natural pest management, crop diversification, and organic soil enrichment.💥 The future of farming is chemical-free, GMO-free, and in the hands of communities—not agribusiness.
