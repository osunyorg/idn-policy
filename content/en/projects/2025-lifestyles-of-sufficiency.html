Policy Objective - Reduce conspicuous consumption and promote public luxury

Shifting from excess to enough. Current economic systems push endless consumption. This policy fosters a culture of sufficiency, community, and mindful living.

Production & Consumption Policy Instruments 👇

Remember: Policy instruments are the means by which governments can achieve the degrowth goals/objectives we urgently need!

1 - From excess to enough: shifting consumption toward sufficiency and shared abundance 🧑‍🤝‍👩🏾

💡 Translation:  A good life isn’t about owning more—it’s about having enough, together. 🏡 Promote minimalism, repair culture, and sufficiency-based consumption. 🤝 Support shared living, community spaces, and collaborative consumption. 💥 Sufficiency is about meeting needs—not keeping up with consumer trends.
